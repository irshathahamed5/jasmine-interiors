Fiberglass Mosquito Net

These fiberglass mosquito nets are widely used as attachable and detachable mosquito mesh, offering an economical, durable, rust-free, and easy-to-wash solution. The Velcro-type tape is a major component in this setup. The fiberglass mesh is cut to the required window or ventilator measurements. The loop of the tape is securely sewn to the four sides of the mesh, while the hook side of the Velcro tape is stapled to the outer wooden frame of the required windows or ventilators.

For aluminum or UPVC windows or ventilators, a sticker-type hook (more expensive than the normal type) is applied using durable SR glue on the outer frame. This ensures robust protection against mosquitoes, flies, and other tiny insects. Our mosquito nets come in three quality meshes: SK Nets, Saint-Gobain, and Phifer, tailored to the Chennai environment.

Magic Screen Features

Velcro Hook Details

The Velcro hook component used in our fiberglass mosquito nets is designed for easy attachment and detachment. It is durable, rust-free, and ensures a secure fit on various window and ventilator frames.

For the head of the window, we use 2.5-inch hooks to provide a stronger hold, while 1-inch hooks are used for the sides to ensure a secure but flexible attachment. The hook side of the Velcro tape is stapled to the outer wooden frame or applied using SR glue for aluminum or UPVC frames.

This versatile hook mechanism provides a reliable barrier against mosquitoes, flies, and other tiny insects while allowing for easy maintenance and cleaning of the mosquito nets. Available in both standard and sticker-type variants.
